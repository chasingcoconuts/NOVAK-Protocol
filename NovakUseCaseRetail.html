NOVAK Use Case: Retail Purchase Integrity (POS)

Securing Transactional Data Against Fraud and Pricing Error

Scenario: During the busy Christmas shopping season, a customer purchases an item using a "Buy-One-Get-One" coupon. The Point-of-Sale (POS) system executes this transaction. NOVAK proves that the final price, the applied discount, and the inventory deduction were done according to the **official store policies**.

1. Capture NIPS Data (Transaction Inputs)

What is captured?
- Item SKU(s) & original price
- Coupon/Discount Code applied
- Final total price & tax rate
- Terminal ID & Clerk ID

This is the raw data surrounding the sale event.

→

2. Generate HARMONEE Receipt (Proof of Execution)

Inputs to HARMONEE:
- NOVAK Rule (e.g., "XMAS_BOGO_DISCOUNT_RULE_V3")
- The captured NIPS Data (the transaction details)
- Current Timestamp

The receipt proves the system correctly applied the official BOGO rule.

→

3. Update REVELATION Chain (Store Audit)

Result:
- The current HARMONEE links into the store's **REVELATION** chain.
- This chain secures every transaction from every register.

The entire day's sales are now an immutable record.

**Result:** NOVAK prevents **Internal Fraud** (e.g., clerks applying unauthorized discounts) and **External Audit Risk** (proving correct sales tax was charged). Auditors can verify the integrity of the store's entire sales record for the holiday season by simply checking the continuous **REVELATION Chain**.
