NOVAK Verification: From History to Proof

How an external party validates the integrity of a single transaction
External Auditor Starts The Trustless Query The auditor chooses a transaction's record and retrieves its stored **REVELATION Hash** and **NIPS Data**.
‚Üì
Input: REVELATION History The Claimed History The auditor receives: **Previous REVELATION Hash**, **NOVAK Rule**, and the **NIPS Data** (Input/Output).
‚Üì
Step 1: Re-Generate HARMONEE The Cryptographic Proof The auditor independently hashes **(Rule + NIPS Data + Timestamp)** to regenerate the expected **HARMONEE** receipt.
‚Üì
Step 2: Re-Generate REVELATION The Chain Link Check The auditor then hashes **(Previous REVELATION Hash + Expected HARMONEE)** to generate the final, expected **REVELATION Hash**.
The expected final hash is compared against the actual **REVELATION Hash** stored in the database.
IF EXPECTED HASH = STORED HASH → **INTEGRITY IS MATHEMATICALLY PROVEN**
NOVAK enables **instant, deterministic audit** of 100% of the governance history, replacing retrospective sampling with mathematical certainty.
