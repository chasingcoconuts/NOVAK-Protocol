Traditional Regulatory Audit Process

Periodic, Sample-Based Verification
TRANSACTION HAPPENS A financial transaction or process decision is executed.
↓
SAMPLING & DISCOVERY (3 MONTHS LATER) External auditors retrieve **a subset** of records and compare them to policy manually.
Goal: Find proof of *non-compliance* (i.e., find a mistake).
↓
AUDIT REPORT A report of findings based on the sampled data. Proves: The sample set *appears* correct at a single point in time.
↓
CORE RISK: TIME & INCOMPLETE COVERAGE Audit is always retrospective (too late). Only a small sample is checked, meaning fraud/mistakes can be missed.

NOVAK Architecture

Continuous, Full-Coverage, Mathematical Attestation
TRANSACTION/RULE EXECUTION (NIPS + NOVAK) The rule and data are executed and immediately hashed.
↓
HARMONEE/REVELATION CHAINING (REAL-TIME) **Every single execution** (100% of data) is instantly stamped and mathematically chained.
Goal: Provide proof of *continuous compliance* (i.e., prove correctness).
↓
VERIFIABLE REVELATION CHAIN An immutable, 100% complete, cryptographic audit trail. Proves: The entire history is mathematically correct and unbroken.
↓
CORE STRENGTH: CONTINUOUS, IMMUTABLE TRUTH Audit is instantaneous. Integrity covers 100% of all data. Tampering is mathematically impossible without breaking the chain.
