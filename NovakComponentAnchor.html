The Four Pillars of Proof

NOVAK’s Core Cryptographic Components in Review

NIPS
The **Novak Immutable Proof System**. This is the digitally signed packet containing the raw data input, timestamp, and context for the transaction.
DATA INPUT
NOVAK RULE
The immutable, versioned, plain-text policy or governance rule against which the execution is judged (e.g., *XMAS_DISCOUNT_RULE_V3*).
GOVERNANCE POLICY
HARMONEE
The **Atomic Proof Receipt**. The cryptographic hash of the NIPS, the NOVAK Rule, and the Previous REVELATION. Proves execution integrity.
ATOMIC RECEIPT
REVELATION
The **Continuous Chain Hash**. The final, perpetually linked hash that secures the integrity of all prior transactions. The source of truth.
IMMUTABLE CHAIN
NIPS + NOVAK Rule → **HARMONEE** (Proof of Execution) → **REVELATION** (Proof of History)
