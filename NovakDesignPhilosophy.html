The NOVAK Design Philosophy

Shifting the Core of Enterprise Trust

Trust Model: Traditional
Source of Trust: Human Authority (Auditors, Admins, Employees)
Integrity Metric: Permissions & Sampling (Verifies 1% of transactions)
Risk Status: Contingent Liability (Reactive, subject to fraud/error)
Evidence Reliability: Mutable Logs (Logs can be edited or deleted)
NOVAK BRIDGE
Trust Model: NOVAK
Source of Trust: Cryptographic Certainty (SHA-256 Math)
Integrity Metric: Continuous Hashing (Verifies 100% of transactions)
Risk Status: Verifiable Asset (Proactive, risk quantified to zero)
Evidence Reliability: Immutable Chain (Tamper-evident, history secured)
NOVAK: The Protocol of Proof NOVAK fundamentally shifts the audit burden from **human scrutiny** to **mathematical integrity**, permanently eliminating the audit gap.
